About Expiry Eye

Expiry Eye is an innovative solution that brings Artificial Intelligence and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) together to solve the issue of expired products. With our system, you can automatically track and manage expiry dates of various products, ensuring that you never miss a deadline again. Our technology scans product labels, extracts relevant information, and notifies users in real-time about the expiry status, helping you stay organized and efficient.

Why Choose Expiry Eye?

  • Efficient Product Management: Never lose track of expiry dates again.
  • Environmentally Friendly: By reducing waste, we contribute to a greener planet.
  • Innovative Technology: We leverage advanced OCR to extract dates and send timely alerts.
